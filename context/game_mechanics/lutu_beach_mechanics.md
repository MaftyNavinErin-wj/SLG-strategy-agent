# 率土之滨 Mechanics Notes

Status: first-pass public-source synthesis  
Access date: 2026-06-27
Latest refresh: 2026-06-27

## One-Line Read

率土之滨是国内赛季制大地图 SLG 的原型级产品。它的核心不是“三国卡牌”本身，而是把武将/战法构筑、真实大地图占地、同盟组织、外交、攻城、资源与赛季重置绑成一个长期战争模拟。后来的三国志战略版和三国谋定天下都可以理解为在它的基础上做不同方向的降摩擦和再包装。

## Core Fantasy

The player is a lord in a persistent seasonal war board. The fantasy is not only "own famous generals"; it is:

1. Build a viable war account from generals, tactics, land, and resources.
2. Expand across a connected map by occupying adjacent land.
3. Join an alliance and participate in collective geopolitical conflict.
4. Win season status through command, discipline, diplomacy, and endurance.

The important product point: 率土 gives players a credible sense of living in a strategic world. Land, roads, passes, cities, distance, stamina, and diplomacy all have consequences.

## Season And Server Loop

The loop is:

1. New season / new map.
2. Early opening: land grabbing, resource ramp, team formation.
3. Alliance consolidation and local expansion.
4. Pass/city timing and inter-alliance conflict.
5. Large-scale state war and diplomatic resolution.
6. Season settlement and account carryover.
7. Next season with new script/rules/meta pressure.

The reset preserves account identity while refreshing the strategic board. Permanent collection and some account development persist; map control, season ranking, and territory state reset. This is the key design solution to SLG stagnation.

## Map And Land

The map is the core system:

1. Land levels determine resource yield and opening risk.
2. Adjacent occupation makes expansion path-dependent.
3. Passes and cities define strategic chokepoints.
4. Distance and marching time create logistics.
5. Alliance territory and front lines create social obligations.
6. Forts, roads, and staging points turn map control into operation planning.

Compared with many COK-like products, the map is not a decorative coordinate field. It is the board on which the season is won or lost.

## Opening / 开荒

开荒 is both a personal skill check and an alliance race.

Core variables:

1. Starting team quality.
2. General level and tactics level.
3. Land difficulty and defender composition.
4. Resource balance.
5. Troop loss and recovery.
6. Stamina and timing.
7. Whether the player's growth matches alliance route/city plans.

Why it matters:

1. Early land income compounds.
2. Opening efficiency decides when the player can contribute to alliance operations.
3. Bad opening wastes resources and delays team transition.
4. The pressure creates high community demand for opening guides and "平民开荒" plans.

## Generals, Tactics, And Buildcraft

The buildcraft stack:

1. Generals: camp, cost, attributes, innate skill, attack distance, role.
2. Tactics / 战法: type, activation, target, damage/control/heal/mitigation.
3. Team structure: front/middle/back, speed order, damage chain, protection, control.
4.兵种 and matchup.
5. Season environment and available card pool.
6. Coexistence across multiple teams.

率土's card depth is high because value is relational. A strong general is not enough; the question is whether the account can assemble coherent teams under cost, distance, and tactic constraints.

## Combat

Combat is mostly pre-battle decision plus probabilistic resolution.

Important factors:

1. Team construction.
2. Formation/position and attack distance.
3. Tactic activation order and target selection.
4. Damage type, control, healing, and reduction.
5. Troop strength and injury/loss.
6. Morale/distance/stamina where applicable.
7. Matchup and counter.

Battle reports are a learning surface. Veteran players read reports to identify whether the issue was team logic, tactic level, sequence luck, counter matchup, or pure account gap.

## Alliance And Diplomacy

The alliance is the center of 率土.

Core alliance jobs:

1. Opening route planning.
2. City/pass timing.
3. Front-line command.
4. Diplomacy and coalition management.
5. Internal discipline.
6. Reward and season expectation management.
7. Migration and long-term member retention.

Roles include leader, commander, diplomat, front-line grinder, scout, high-power breaker, logistics/support, and reliable attendance member.

Design read: 率土 makes alliance leadership extremely valuable because the map has real operational complexity. This is why the game can sustain identity beyond individual card ownership.

## Monetization

Main surfaces:

1. General acquisition.
2. Tactic completion and inheritance pressure.
3. Season-limited/meta-relevant cards.
4. Resource and acceleration convenience.
5. Multi-team coexistence and account depth.

The monetization is horizontal-combinatorial rather than purely vertical. A payer buys more possibilities and faster completion, but must still translate the card box into teams and season value.

## Current / Latest Public Snapshot, 2026-06-27

Current public materials indicate 率土 is still operating as a mature, deep-season SLG:

1. Official and guide sites continue to frame the game around real-time multiplayer strategy, season choice, generals/tactics, and alliance war.
2. 2026 update/search results point to ongoing scenario/theme refreshes, including 蚩尤讨炎黄 and related season updates in guide coverage.
3. Player community content remains concentrated around 开荒, 配将,赛季选择, account value, and alliance/server ecology.
4. The game is now a reference object: when discussing later products, "像不像率土" usually means map geopolitics, alliance command, season reset, and horizontal team buildcraft.

Answering rule:

1. For product design questions, treat 率土 as the baseline for 率土 Like.
2. For exact current team/meta advice, verify season, server stage, card box, and current patch.
3. For comparison with 三国志战略版, focus on origin versus friction reduction.
4. For comparison with 三国谋定天下, focus on depth versus operational accessibility.

## What To Know For Q&A

1. 率土's core asset is not just the card system; it is the strategic credibility of the map.
2. The season reset is what keeps geopolitics and account value from hard-freezing.
3. Alliance leaders are central because the map creates real coordination work.
4. Card depth monetizes through combinations and coexistence, not only raw power.
5. Its friction is part of its identity, but also the opportunity later products attack.
