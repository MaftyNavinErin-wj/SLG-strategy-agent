# 三国谋定天下 Mechanics Notes

Status: first-pass public-source synthesis  
Access date: 2026-06-27
Latest refresh: 2026-06-27

## One-Line Read

三国谋定天下是新世代 率土 Like / 三国 SLG 的降摩擦产品。它保留赛季、大地图、同盟、攻城、武将战法和开荒这些核心，但通过职业系统、自动铺路/低操作负担、辎重、军演和更轻的成长节奏，把传统率土系的认知和操作压力往下压。

## Core Fantasy

The player fantasy is still "Three Kingdoms lord in alliance war," but the product adds a clearer role fantasy:

1. I am not only a generic lord; I can choose an occupation / career role.
2. My account can contribute through profession, logistics, development, or battle.
3. I can participate in alliance war with lower operational burden than older 率土-like products.

This is important because it attacks a known weakness of deep SLG: ordinary players often feel they are just weaker soldiers under commanders. A career system gives them a named contribution path.

## Season And Core Loop

The core loop:

1. Choose season / server environment.
2. Start opening with guided land progression.
3. Build teams from generals and tactics.
4. Use profession systems and city/internal development to specialize.
5. Join alliance operations: expansion, siege, war, defense.
6. Participate in seasonal objectives and settlement.
7. Carry account value into later seasons with new rules/meta.

The product does not abandon season SLG. It tries to make the season loop more accessible.

## Career / Profession System

The occupation system is one of the main differentiators.

Public guides frequently discuss professions such as:

1. 镇军.
2. 神行.
3. 奇佐.
4. 青囊.
5. 天工.
6. 司仓.

Exact names and balance may change by season, but the design role is stable:

1. Create differentiated player contribution.
2. Give non-whales useful jobs.
3. Reduce pure card-power hierarchy.
4. Support alliance logistics, mobility, healing/support, construction, or resource roles.
5. Make season planning more class-like and MMO-like.

Design read: 三国谋定天下 is trying to make "alliance role" visible in the system rather than relying only on social command.

## Map, Land, And Movement

The game keeps the 率土-like importance of map and land, but reduces friction:

1. Land occupation and resource growth remain core.
2. Big-map expansion and alliance war remain the strategic board.
3. Automated or simplified paving/pathing reduces repetitive operations.
4. Logistics concepts such as辎重 and city/territory systems create strategic constraints without requiring every player to manually micromanage as much.

The design intent is not "less strategy"; it is "less manual tax." The risk is that reducing friction can also reduce the sense of hard-earned map control if not compensated by profession, alliance, or seasonal objectives.

## Opening / 开荒

Opening is still important, but the product tries to make it more approachable.

Core variables:

1. Early generals and tactics.
2. Recommended lineups and lower-loss land progression.
3. Profession choice and early resource needs.
4. Automated guidance / reduced operation load.
5. Transition from opening team to PVP teams.

Player-facing questions remain familiar:

1. Which starter team should I use?
2. Which profession should I choose?
3. How do I open without high-rarity generals?
4. When do I switch from opening to war teams?

## Generals, Tactics, And Combat

The buildcraft remains close to 三国 SLG logic:

1. Generals provide attributes, roles, and native skills.
2. Tactics / 战法 define damage, control, healing, defense, and tempo.
3. Team composition and skill order matter.
4.兵种, counter, and battle report interpretation still matter.
5. Season meta and available cards change recommendations.

The difference from older products is not that buildcraft disappears. It is that the surrounding UX, profession systems, and season guidance try to make buildcraft less punishing for ordinary players.

## Alliance And War

The alliance remains the long-term retention center:

1. Alliance expansion and siege.
2. Scheduled operations.
3. Profession-based coordination.
4. Logistics and supply.
5. Diplomacy and state/server identity.
6. Season settlement and migration.

Because professions create formal roles, alliance command can assign work more legibly. This is a major design difference from older products where many roles are purely social and invisible in mechanics.

## Monetization

Main surfaces:

1. General acquisition and card pool.
2. Tactic completion.
3. Growth acceleration and seasonal resources.
4. Profession/season-related optimization pressure.
5. Account depth for multiple teams and roles.

The commercial model is still card/buildcraft + season retention, but the product's selling point is lower friction and broader participation rather than only higher strategic purity.

## What To Know For Q&A

1. 三国谋定天下 is not just a lighter 三国志战略版. Its strongest differentiator is role/profession formalization.
2. It attacks the accessibility problem of deep season SLG: too much manual operation, opaque contribution, and early frustration.
3. The big design bet is that lowering friction can expand the audience without collapsing alliance-war depth.
4. Its map and alliance still matter; the product is not a pure card battler.
5. The key evaluation question is whether profession/logistics systems create real strategic depth or mainly reduce pain.
