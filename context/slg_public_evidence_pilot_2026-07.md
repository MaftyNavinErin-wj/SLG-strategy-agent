# SLG Public Evidence Pilot: From Structural Hypotheses To Observable Outputs

Status: first public-information pilot; directional, not an operating dashboard  
Evidence cut-off: 2026-07-24  
Benchmark products: Lords Mobile / 王国纪元, Whiteout Survival / 无尽冬日, Rise of Kingdoms / 万国觉醒, 三国：谋定天下, 重返帝国

## Purpose

This pilot tests how far the current structural framework can be connected to observable business outputs before internal data access exists.

It does **not** attempt to manufacture a complete model from incomplete public data. It separates four evidence states:

1. **Fact**: publisher filing, earnings release, official store, or official rule.
2. **Proxy**: a broader company / portfolio metric or a third-party estimate that is directionally relevant but not identical to the target metric.
3. **Inference**: a causal interpretation produced by the A x G x R and power-structure framework.
4. **Blank**: a field that public information does not support reliably enough.

The current causal stack is:

> entry / theme -> Tier-0 A x G x R implemented through Tier-1 systems -> power and social structure -> compatible monetization -> Tier-2 optimization -> observed outputs.

Entry and theme are adjacent research modules, not new AGR axes or numbered tiers.

Public information can currently validate the two ends of this chain better than the middle: product rules and high-level scale are visible; asset-level spend, payer distribution, cohort retention, alliance ecology, and causal attribution are mostly hidden.

## Executive Read

1. **Casual COK is the strongest publicly validated business pattern.** Whiteout Survival combines unusually strong early retention with extreme scale and multi-year revenue growth. This confirms the Derrick deck's judgment that casual entry does not imply shallow monetization. It does not yet prove which backend feature causes the monetization depth.
2. **Classic COK shows decay with exceptional monetizable longevity.** Lords Mobile's MAU and revenue have declined, but a ten-year-old product still produced HK$2.17bn in 2025. The structural claim should therefore be “mature vertical systems can preserve a high-value core,” not simply “classic COK has weak retention.”
3. **ROK remains a durable premium niche, but public data does not cleanly distinguish its payer or retention structure from classic COK.** Current store-intelligence snapshots show meaningful revenue eight years after launch, while the strongest differentiator remains the real-time war experience and its operating burden.
4. **Lutu Like can have a short effective-war window and a much longer commercial window at the same time.** Bilibili's game-revenue series, repeatedly attributed primarily to 三国：谋定天下, remained high through 2025 despite the Derrick deck's day-15 Sili / late-season filler diagnosis. “The season is strategically solved early” is therefore not equivalent to “the season stops monetizing.”
5. **Return-to-Empire-style fusion remains the least publicly provable profile.** Tencent disclosed a strong launch and 2022 revenue contribution, but not title-level retention, payer, or later lifecycle data. The deck's double-pressure diagnosis remains a good hypothesis, not a publicly demonstrated causal result.

## Product Evidence Cards

### 1. Classic COK — Lords Mobile / 王国纪元

**Public facts**

1. IGG reported 2024 Lords Mobile revenue of HK$2.589bn and 9.0m MAU as of year-end 2024.
2. MAU was 7.7m as of June 2025.
3. Full-year 2025 revenue was HK$2.165bn, down about 16% year over year, while the product reached its tenth year.
4. The official direct store exposes a very wide price ladder, including US$0.99 through US$499.99 diamond packages and recurring passes.

**What this supports**

1. The product is declining, but the monetizable tail is exceptionally long.
2. Persistent vertical account power plus recurring resources, acceleration, passes, and live-ops renewal can sustain a large mature revenue base.
3. A crude revenue / point-MAU proxy is broadly stable between 2024 and mid-2025, but it must not be presented as audited ARPU because the numerator is annual recognized revenue and the denominator is point-in-time MAU.

**Still blank**

Payer rate, ARPPU, top-payer concentration, new-server versus old-server retention, kingdom survival, and spend by power / recovery / status object.

Sources: [IGG 2024 annual results presentation](https://statics-global.igg.com/investor/webcasts/2025/03/28/2024%20Annual%20Results%20PPT-Eng.pdf), [IGG 2025 interim results presentation](https://statics-global.igg.com/investor/webcasts/2025/08/29/2025%20Interim%20Results%20PPT-Eng-%E7%9B%B4%E6%92%AD.pdf), [IGG 2025 results release](https://statics.9458.com/game/9900/article/2026/03/25/041543_69c3a7bfdb4076266.pdf), [official diamond store](https://lordsmobile.igg.com/third_payment/?lang=en)

### 2. Casual COK — Whiteout Survival / 无尽冬日

**Public facts and strong proxies**

1. A Sensor Tower retention cohort cited by Naavik estimated US iOS D1 / D7 / D30 retention at 42% / 17% / 8%.
2. Century Huatong reported that Whiteout Survival passed 300m cumulative global downloads by the end of 2025.
3. Sensor Tower ranked Whiteout Survival the second-highest-grossing mobile game globally in 2025; it returned to number one in June 2026.
4. Century Huatong's internet-game revenue reached RMB36.237bn in 2025, up 72.84%; this is a portfolio number, not title revenue, but the filing names Whiteout Survival and Kingshot as core growth engines.

**What this supports**

1. Casual entry can materially improve the top of funnel and early retention without sacrificing backend monetization scale.
2. The strongest causal read is a two-stage product: low-friction survival / management attachment first, then COK-like alliance obligation and vertical spend.
3. The Derrick deck's casual-COK share shift is consistent with current public evidence and is not merely a download-volume story.

**Still blank**

Title-level MAU, payer rate, ARPPU, whale concentration, retention after alliance absorption, server consolidation, and the percentage of users who cross from casual front end into war backend.

Sources: [Century Huatong 2025 annual report summary](https://static.cninfo.com.cn/finalpage/2026-04-30/1225261914.PDF), [Sensor Tower State of Gaming 2026](https://sensortower.com/press/press-release-sensor-tower-state-of-gaming-gaming-drove-52-billion-downloads-82b-iap-revenue-on-mobile-and-12b-premium-revenue-on-steam), [Sensor Tower June 2026 ranking](https://develop.sensortower.com/blog/top-10-worldwide-mobile-games-by-revenue-and-downloads-in-june-2026), [Naavik retention comparison sourced to Sensor Tower](https://naavik.co/digest/how-last-war-is-winning-the-4x-game/)

### 3. ROK — Rise of Kingdoms / 万国觉醒

**Public facts and proxies**

1. The product launched globally in 2018 and official regional communications reported more than 100m global downloads by 2023.
2. Current Sensor Tower app snapshots still estimate several million US dollars of monthly mobile-store revenue per platform in 2026. These are estimates and exclude / incompletely capture direct web stores.
3. Sensor Tower's US Q1 2025 view showed a declining but still meaningful active base and relatively steady weekly revenue during the quarter.
4. The official store description continues to foreground a shared real-time world, free march, gathering, building, and battle rather than connected-land occupation.

**What this supports**

1. ROK is not only a launch-era spectacle; it has durable long-tail monetization.
2. It remains reasonable to separate ROK from both classic COK and Lutu Like because real-time multi-march expression changes activity spikes, online burden, and capacity demand.
3. Public outputs do not yet prove whether ROK's multi-team demand creates meaningful answer-horizontal monetization or mostly capacity-horizontal / vertical throughput monetization.

**Still blank**

Global MAU, payer rate, ARPPU, spend concentration, KvK cohort retention, migration effects, kingdom governance health, and spend split across commanders, equipment, speedups, healing, and capacity.

Sources: [Lilith product page](https://www.lilith.com/en/), [official Google Play listing](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lilithgame.roc.gp), [official 100m-download communication](https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000005.000113144.html), [Sensor Tower Q1 2025 US analysis](https://sensortower.com/blog/2025-q1-unified-top-5-historical%20games-revenue-us-60188699241bc16eb8409802), [Sensor Tower iOS app snapshot](https://app.sensortower.com/overview/1354260888?country=US), [Sensor Tower Google Play app snapshot](https://app.sensortower.com/overview/com.lilithgame.roc.gp)

### 4. Lutu Like — 三国：谋定天下

**Public facts and proxies**

1. Bilibili said the game became its fastest-operated title to reach RMB1bn in gross revenue after the June 2024 launch.
2. Bilibili mobile-game revenue rose to RMB1.823bn in Q3 2024 and RMB1.80bn in Q4 2024, with management primarily attributing the increase to 三国：谋定天下.
3. The same segment recorded RMB1.73bn, RMB1.61bn, RMB1.51bn, and RMB1.54bn in the four quarters of 2025. Full-year 2025 mobile-game revenue was RMB6.39bn, up 14%, primarily driven by the full-year contribution of 三国：谋定天下; Q4 also included Escape from Duckov.
4. Q1 2026 mobile-game revenue was RMB1.52bn. Bilibili described 三国：谋定天下 as transitioning into a stable and mature lifecycle.
5. These figures are Bilibili segment revenue, not title revenue. They are best treated as a high-quality lifecycle proxy because the company repeatedly identifies the title as the main driver.

**What this supports**

1. The product retained a large commercial base well beyond launch and across repeated seasons.
2. The deck's short effective-war-period diagnosis is compatible with strong seasonal monetization. Revenue can be renewed through season starts, roster progression, celebration events, and retained social cohorts even when map suspense decays early.
3. This strengthens the need to separate at least three clocks: acquisition / early retention, effective competitive days, and commercial season renewal.

**Still blank**

Title MAU / DAU, exact title revenue, payer rate, ARPPU, revenue by season day, card breadth versus red-depth spend, alliance-conditioned retention, officer churn, and the revenue impact of an early-decided season.

Sources: [Bilibili Q2 2024 conference call](https://ir.bilibili.com/media/0zsdl0pl/bili-2q-2024-conference-call-script.pdf), [Bilibili Q2 2024 results](https://ir.bilibili.com/media/qcemvd2g/bilibili-inc-announces-second-quarter-2024-financial-results.pdf), [Bilibili Q3 2024 results](https://ir.bilibili.com/media/lg2ixrqm/bilibili-inc-announces-third-quarter-2024-financial-results.pdf), [Bilibili Q4 / FY2024 results](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/02/20/3029366/0/en/Bilibili-Inc-Announces-Fourth-Quarter-and-Fiscal-Year-2024-Financial-Results.html), [Bilibili Q1 2025 results](https://ir.bilibili.com/media/apkmmmog/bilibili-inc-announces-first-quarter-2025-financial-results.pdf), [Bilibili Q2 2025 results](https://ir.bilibili.com/media/vwdbomil/bilibili-inc-announces-second-quarter-2025-financial-results.pdf), [Bilibili Q3 2025 results](https://ir.bilibili.com/media/krmdk0ls/bilibili-inc-announces-third-quarter-2025-financial-results.pdf), [Bilibili Q4 / FY2025 results](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/03/05/3249914/0/en/bilibili-inc-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results.html), [Bilibili Q1 2026 results](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/05/19/3297243/0/en/bilibili-inc-announces-first-quarter-2026-financial-results.html)

### 5. Fusion — 重返帝国

**Public facts**

1. Tencent launched the game in March 2022 and disclosed that it ranked second by gross receipts within its category in China in April 2022.
2. Tencent continued to name it as a contributor to new domestic-game revenue in Q2, Q3, and full-year 2022.
3. Tencent did not provide title-level revenue, retention, payer, or mature-lifecycle metrics in these disclosures.

**What this supports**

1. The product had real launch demand; the fusion thesis did not fail at initial market interest.
2. The public record cannot demonstrate that real-time x season reset caused weaker durability. The Derrick deck's estimated first-month / first-year revenue and double-pressure diagnosis remain the stronger dated internal benchmark.
3. The correct status is “structurally plausible causal hypothesis with outcome evidence,” not “proven structural law.”

**Still blank**

Nearly every operating output after launch: active base, retention, payer distribution, ARPPU, mature revenue, alliance ecology, effective-war duration, and spend split.

Sources: [Tencent Q1 2022 results](https://static.www.tencent.com/uploads/2022/05/18/9a46878abd6009cacd79f241a814442b.pdf), [Tencent Q2 2022 results](https://static.www.tencent.com/uploads/2022/08/17/edc989fb139819b8e6c024abde716ca0.pdf), [Tencent Q3 2022 results](https://static.www.tencent.com/uploads/2022/11/16/34c3cf298649e8f63e2377fe902a4818.pdf), [Tencent 2022 annual report](https://static.www.tencent.com/uploads/2023/04/06/eac54c79c67d8a501bc4b65ff1718223.pdf)

## Comparison With The Derrick Benchmark

### Confirmed

1. Casual COK is not a shallow-monetization branch; it is the strongest current scale pattern.
2. Classic COK retains extraordinary long-tail revenue even while its active base declines.
3. 三国：谋定天下 is a commercially important Lutu-like benchmark rather than only a low-cost acquisition experiment.
4. Return-to-Empire-style fusion has demonstrated launch appeal but weaker publicly visible long-term proof.

### Refined

1. “Retention” must be split into new-user retention, active-account persistence, alliance-conditioned retention, and seasonal reactivation. Public sources usually expose at most one.
2. “Season length” must be split into content season, effective competitive window, commercial renewal window, and social-group lifetime.
3. Revenue durability is not proof of healthy server ecology. A shrinking core or concentrated payer base can still produce high revenue.

### Not Yet Proven

1. Horizontal power creates a thicker mid-payer layer than vertical power.
2. Vertical power necessarily creates higher ARPPU.
3. Fusion underperformance is caused mainly by stacked A / G / R taxes rather than execution, IP, UA, balance, or timing.
4. Ordinary-player organization value produces higher cross-season retention than body value.

## What An Internal Data Interface Should Eventually Request

The first useful data request should be narrow rather than universal:

1. Season-day DAU, playtime, revenue, payer conversion, and ARPPU.
2. Spend decomposition into magnitude, recovery / throughput, capacity, optionality / counters, renewal / depreciation, and status.
3. D1 / D7 / D30 plus S1-to-S2 and S2-to-S3 retention by spend tier, alliance survival, and role.
4. Effective-war end date versus content-season end date.
5. Alliance absorption rate, leader / officer churn, and correlated member churn.
6. Revenue concentration at top 0.1%, 1%, 10%, middle spenders, and low spenders.

Until those fields exist, public research should continue to update lifecycle facts and causal hypotheses, but should not pretend to measure payer economics.
