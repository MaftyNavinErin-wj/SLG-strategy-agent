# SLG Representative Games Research Findings

Status: working synthesis  
Access date: 2026-06-27  
Covered products: 无尽冬日, 率土之滨, 三国志战略版, 三国谋定天下

## Executive Thesis

The representative products show that mobile SLG is not one formula. The important split is structural:

1. 无尽冬日 represents Casual COK Like: casual/survival entry plus vertical power and alliance-event discipline.
2. 率土之滨 represents the original deep 率土 Like baseline: season map, land geopolitics, alliance command, diplomacy, and horizontal card/tactics buildcraft.
3. 三国志战略版 represents mainstreamed 率土 Like: it keeps season geopolitics and card buildcraft, while making the Three Kingdoms fantasy and user experience more accessible.
4. 三国谋定天下 represents low-friction / role-formalized 率土 Like: it tries to preserve season-map depth while reducing manual burden and making player contribution more explicit through professions.

## Method Conclusions

### 1. Sources Must Be Layered

No single source type is enough.

1. Official pages are best for rules, update names, and schedule.
2. Wikis/databases are best for structured system vocabulary.
3. TapTap and guide sites reveal practical player strategy.
4. Zhihu and industry media help interpret product model and category meaning.
5. Tieba is most useful for live pain points: account value, alliance drama, opening frustration, lineup diagnosis, and churn.

The research method should store distilled claims, URLs, reliability, and open questions, not raw copied community content.

### 2. Separate Stable Mechanics From Live Meta

Stable mechanics can be used for product/design Q&A. Live meta must be date-stamped and re-browsed.

Examples:

1. 无尽冬日's furnace/alliance/power-ladder model is stable.
2. Its current best heroes depend on state age and hero generation.
3. 三国志战略版's season-map/card-buildcraft model is stable.
4. Its current teams depend on script, patch, account box, and tactics.
5. 三国谋定天下's profession-system positioning is stable.
6. Exact profession balance needs current season verification.

### 3. Mechanism Research Should Explain Player Behavior

A system note is incomplete if it only says what the feature is. It should explain:

1. What bottleneck it creates.
2. What routine it teaches.
3. What alliance behavior it demands.
4. What spending pressure it creates.
5. What non-paying players can still optimize.
6. What community questions it generates.

## Product Conclusions

### 无尽冬日

Structural read:

1. Survival theme is an onboarding and substitution strategy, not just a skin.
2. Furnace is the early account anchor.
3. Alliance is the retention anchor.
4. State/SvS and alliance events are the endgame anchor.
5. Monetization is vertical: speedups, resources, heroes, gear, troop tiers, Fire Crystal / Flame Tech, and event-limited resources.

Important mechanism insight:

The game converts broad casual users into scheduled SLG players. The early survival loop lowers resistance; later alliance events enforce attendance, hierarchy, and optimization.

Q&A rule:

For current advice, ask server age, unlocked hero generation, spend tier, target mode, and whether the player is rally lead or joiner.

### 率土之滨

Structural read:

1. It is the baseline for 率土 Like.
2. The map is the product's strategic object, not a backdrop.
3. Land, adjacency, passes, cities, routes, diplomacy, and season reset create long-term depth.
4. Card/tactics monetization is horizontal-combinatorial.
5. Alliance leadership is central because the map creates real operational work.

Important mechanism insight:

率土's friction is not accidental. It is part of the depth that creates strategic credibility, but it also creates the accessibility gap later products try to solve.

Q&A rule:

For current advice, verify season/script, server stage, card box, tactics, and whether the question is about opening, war, account value, or comparison.

### 三国志战略版

Structural read:

1. It is a mainstream accessible 率土 Like.
2. The season loop, land expansion, alliance war, and card/tactics buildcraft remain central.
3. Three Kingdoms theme and more approachable UX broaden the audience.
4. Battle reports and 配将 form a large community knowledge loop.
5. Monetization sells generals, tactics completion, and team options more than a simple vertical ladder.

Important mechanism insight:

三战's depth is distributed across opening efficiency, map routes, alliance command, and battle-report/team interpretation. It is not just "抽武将打架."

Q&A rule:

For current advice, identify season/script, account box, tactics availability, and target use case: 开荒, PVP, 驻守, 攻城, or共存.

### 三国谋定天下

Structural read:

1. It is a new-generation low-friction 率土 Like.
2. Its key differentiator is profession / occupation formalization.
3. It keeps season, map, alliance, siege, generals, and tactics.
4. It reduces operation burden through stronger guidance and simplified/automated pathing.
5. It tries to make non-whale contribution more visible through system roles and logistics.

Important mechanism insight:

三谋's bet is that the audience for deep season SLG can expand if contribution is clearer and manual pain is lower. The risk is whether reduced friction weakens the hard-earned map-control identity that makes 率土-like products durable.

Q&A rule:

For current advice, verify version/region, season, profession, account box, and whether the target is opening, alliance war, logistics, PVP, or product comparison.

## Cross-Product Conclusions

### 1. Alliance Is The Real Retention Structure

All four products depend on alliance, but alliance means different things:

1. 无尽冬日: alliance turns casual entrants into scheduled event participants.
2. 率土之滨: alliance is the operational and diplomatic heart.
3. 三国志战略版: alliance is a season war organization.
4. 三国谋定天下: alliance is a war organization with more formalized player roles.

### 2. Depth Can Come From Different Places

SLG depth may be:

1. Vertical account growth.
2. Rally hierarchy.
3. Seasonal map geopolitics.
4. Card/tactics buildcraft.
5. Battle-report interpretation.
6. Profession/logistics roles.
7. Diplomacy and alliance leadership.

Do not evaluate all SLG by the same depth dimension.

### 3. Non-Whale Contribution Is A Core Design Problem

Winning SLG must give ordinary players socially recognized jobs.

1. 无尽冬日 gives attendance, joining, reinforcing, and event participation.
2. 率土 gives front-line execution, scouting, logistics, diplomacy, and discipline.
3. 三国志战略版 gives opening contribution, route execution, siege attendance, and team specialization.
4. 三国谋定天下 tries to systematize these jobs through professions and logistics.

### 4. Community Knowledge Is Part Of The Product

Frequent questions reveal hidden complexity:

1. 无尽冬日: hero generation, rally joiner setup, event optimization.
2. 率土之滨: opening, team construction, season choice, alliance politics.
3. 三国志战略版: 配将, 战报诊断, 开荒, script/meta changes.
4. 三国谋定天下: profession choice, opening teams, logistics, season-specific rules.

The community is not external noise. It is a mechanism translation layer.

## How To Use These Findings

For product questions:

1. Start from the mountain: Casual COK Like or 率土 Like.
2. Identify the system being discussed: growth, map, combat, alliance, season/event, monetization.
3. Explain the player behavior and bottleneck the system creates.
4. Compare to the nearest representative product.
5. Flag live-meta uncertainty when relevant.

For strategy or BD questions:

1. Ask what audience expansion problem the product solves.
2. Ask what depth it preserves or sacrifices.
3. Ask whether non-whales have meaningful roles.
4. Ask whether alliance leaders are helped or burdened.
5. Ask whether monetization fits the product's depth source.
