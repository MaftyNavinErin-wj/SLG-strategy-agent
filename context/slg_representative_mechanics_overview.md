## Category Positioning

无尽冬日, 率土之滨, 三国志战略版, and 三国谋定天下 are all mobile SLG, but they sit on different positions in the category.

| Dimension | 无尽冬日 / Whiteout Survival | 率土之滨 | 三国志战略版 | 三国谋定天下 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mountain | Casual COK Like | Original / baseline 率土 Like | Mainstream accessible 率土 Like | New-generation low-friction 率土 Like |
| Entry fantasy | Survive the cold, build a settlement | Become a lord on a real geopolitical map | Three Kingdoms lord with card/team strategy | Three Kingdoms lord with formal career role |
| First-hour hook | Furnace, survivors, facilities, PVE | Land opening and map expansion | Land opening, generals, guided growth | Occupation choice, guided opening, lower manual burden |
| Strategic center | Alliance event discipline and rally hierarchy | Season map, routes, diplomacy, alliance command | Season map, land routes, alliance command, team buildcraft | Season map plus profession/logistics and accessibility |
| Power model | More vertical: hero, troops, gear, research, speedups | Horizontal-combinatorial card/tactics depth | Horizontal card/tactics depth with stronger mainstream UX | Horizontal buildcraft plus profession contribution |
| Map role | War/event stage and state ecology | Core strategy board | Core strategy board | Core board, but with reduced operation tax |
| Social role | Alliance converts casual entrants into scheduled SLG players | Alliance is the product's heart | Alliance is war organization and season identity | Alliance assigns both war and profession/logistics roles |
| Reset/renewal | State lifecycle, events, hero generations, cross-state war | Season reset and new geopolitical board | Season reset, scripts, new meta, repeated opening race | Season reset plus occupation/script iteration |
| Monetization feel | Acceleration and vertical power ladder | Card/tactics/team possibilities | Card pool, tactics completion, team options | Card/buildcraft plus role and season optimization |

## The Core Difference

无尽冬日 asks:

> Can broad casual/survival traffic be converted into alliance-centered SLG monetization?

率土之滨 asks:

> Can a credible seasonal war board sustain long-term social strategy and card monetization?

三国志战略版 asks:

> Can seasonal geopolitics and card buildcraft keep strategic players re-entering the war board?

三国谋定天下 asks:

> Can 率土-like depth be widened through lower friction, formal player roles, and clearer contribution?

This difference explains most mechanic choices.

## System Comparison

### 1. Onboarding

无尽冬日:

1. Starts with emotional clarity: the furnace keeps people alive.
2. Uses buildings, survivors, heroes, and PVE to make growth legible.
3. Delays the full social-war burden until the player has joined an alliance and learned the schedule.

三国志战略版:

1. Starts closer to the core SLG problem: occupy land, grow resource income, form teams.
2. Requires earlier understanding of loss, land level, troop count, and team suitability.
3. The first meaningful skill expression is 开荒.

率土之滨:

1. Starts closest to the raw season-map problem: land, adjacency, resource, alliance route.
2. High early friction is part of the product's identity and depth.
3. New players often need guides or alliance mentorship to understand why each action matters.

三国谋定天下:

1. Starts from the same map/season base, but adds profession choice and more guidance.
2. Reduces repetitive operation burden through simplified or automated paving/pathing.
3. Gives players a named role earlier, so contribution is more legible.

Takeaway: 无尽冬日 hides SLG complexity behind survival progression. 率土之滨 exposes the raw strategic board. 三国志战略版 mainstreams that model. 三国谋定天下 lowers operation and role-friction further.

### 2. Growth

无尽冬日:

1. Furnace and building level gate the account.
2. Troop tier and quantity create obvious power milestones.
3. Heroes and gear create recurring premium goals.
4. Event rewards and packs accelerate the ladder.

三国志战略版:

1. Account value comes from generals, tactics, and accumulated team options.
2. Land/resource growth matters each season but is reset or re-contested.
3. Buildcraft knowledge converts card ownership into war value.
4. Strong accounts are flexible, not merely high-numbered.

率土之滨:

1. Account value is card/tactics depth plus the ability to field coherent teams across seasons.
2. Seasonal land growth is temporary, but account knowledge and alliance reputation persist.
3. Growth is deliberately entangled with map timing and alliance plans.

三国谋定天下:

1. Growth includes generals/tactics, but also profession value and logistics contribution.
2. The product tries to make non-whale contribution more visible through system roles.
3. Seasonal iteration can change profession and team priorities.

Takeaway: 无尽冬日 has a clearer numerical ladder. The three 三国 products have more combinatorial ladders, with 率土 as the deepest/highest-friction baseline and 三国谋定天下 as the most role-formalized variant.

### 3. Combat

无尽冬日:

1. Combat is heavily influenced by rally leader power and account stats.
2. Strategy sits in preparation: hero choice, troop mix, buff timing, joiner discipline.
3. Battle events often test attendance and coordination more than tactical originality.

三国志战略版:

1. Combat is a battle-report puzzle: generals, tactics, morale,兵种克制, probability, and matchup.
2. Team construction has high expressive value.
3. Map logistics and morale make where/how far you fight strategically relevant.

率土之滨:

1. Combat is deeply tied to team structure, distance, tactics sequence, map location, and season context.
2. Battle-report reading is central to player learning.
3. The cost of bad decisions is high because opening and war losses affect the alliance board.

三国谋定天下:

1. Combat keeps the generals/tactics/report logic.
2. Profession and logistics systems can affect when and how teams contribute.
3. The design target is to keep meaningful team theory while reducing surrounding operation pain.

Takeaway: 无尽冬日 combat is alliance execution over a vertical power base. The 三国 map-SLG products use team theory plus map context; 三国谋定天下 adds profession/logistics as an extra layer.

### 4. Alliance

无尽冬日:

1. Alliance gives protection, help, rallies, event rewards, and social scheduling.
2. Whales and rally leaders are highly central.
3. Ordinary players contribute by attendance, correct joining, reinforcement, and event participation.

三国志战略版:

1. Alliance is a command organization.
2. Officers, diplomats, planners, and executors all matter.
3. The map gives leadership real strategic work: routes, pass timing, city priority, defense.

率土之滨:

1. Alliance is the heart of the product.
2. Commanders and diplomats matter because map routes, fronts, and season settlement are genuinely strategic.
3. Social hierarchy is reinforced by operational competence, not only spending.

三国谋定天下:

1. Alliance still commands war, but professions make player roles more visible.
2. Logistics, support, movement, and construction-like contributions can be system-recognized.
3. The goal is to make ordinary players easier to organize and retain.

Takeaway: all four need alliance, but the job differs. 无尽冬日 turns casual players into a disciplined event body. 率土/三战 turn players into a geopolitical army. 三国谋定天下 tries to make that army's roles more explicit.

### 5. Long-Term Retention

无尽冬日 retention anchors:

1. State identity.
2. Alliance membership.
3. Hero generations and power chase.
4. Cross-state conflict.
5. Event calendar habits.

三国志战略版 retention anchors:

1. Season reset and new board state.
2. Card/team collection goals.
3. Alliance honor and migration.
4. Script/meta change.
5. Battle-report learning and community discussion.

率土之滨 retention anchors:

1. Season reset and long-running alliance identity.
2. Deep map war memory and diplomacy.
3. Card/tactics account progression.
4. Veteran community knowledge and leadership networks.

三国谋定天下 retention anchors:

1. Season reset and script/profession iteration.
2. Lower operation burden that supports broader participation.
3. Occupation identity and alliance contribution.
4. Team progression and current meta adaptation.

Takeaway: 无尽冬日 refreshes through event/state/power cadence. 率土 and 三国志战略版 refresh through season and meta recombination. 三国谋定天下 refreshes through season plus profession/accessibility iteration.

## Design Lessons

1. "SLG depth" is not one thing. It can be social scheduling, map geopolitics, team buildcraft, rally discipline, or server diplomacy.
2. Casual entry works when it has a credible path into alliance obligation. Otherwise it remains a separate mini-game.
3. Season reset works when it preserves paid/account identity while resetting strategic board state.
4. Battle reports are a design surface. If players can debate why they won/lost, the community becomes part of the meta engine.
5. Alliance leaders are unpaid live-ops staff. Systems that help or hurt them directly affect retention.
6. The strongest SLG products make non-whales useful. They may not beat whales, but they must have socially recognized jobs.

## Agent Answering Rules

When asked about these games:

1. Always identify which mountain the question belongs to: Casual COK Like or 率土 Like.
2. Separate player-facing fantasy from hidden retention/monetization role.
3. Explain mechanics through loops: individual growth, alliance obligation, map/event objective, reset/renewal.
4. Call out uncertainty when a question depends on current patch, current hero generation, or current season script.
5. For current meta, browse again before answering. These systems change and community consensus moves quickly.
